What Is This?

The Lotus Chart is a 3-dimensional data view capable of showing actionable metadata for over 2000 records on-screen at the same time.

Reading the Lotus Chart:   

Each chart link shows a different symbol. The links that are tallest and closest to the center show the items with the highest daily trading volume. Links that are shorter and in the outer rings show items with lower volume.

Hover over a chart link to see item info. Click the link to open a new window with further details. Use the mouse wheel to zoom in or out.
